Privacy Policy - FG Insights Product Sourcing Agent

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

FG Insights LLC ("we," "us," "our," or "FG Insights") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Product Sourcing Agent (the "Service").

By connecting your Instagram account, Gmail, and other platforms to our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our Service.

Quick Summary: Our Service analyzes your Instagram Direct Messages, Gmail, TikTok content, and YouTube videos to identify products and topics that resonate with your audience. We use AI to process this data and generate personalized product recommendations for your content creation. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Instagram Data

When you connect your Instagram Business or Creator account to our Service, we collect and process the following information:

  • Instagram Direct Messages (DMs): Message content, sender usernames, profile pictures, and timestamps from messages sent to your Instagram account
  • Instagram Profile Information: Your Instagram username, profile picture, bio, and account type (Business/Creator)
  • Instagram Post Data: Public posts, engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares), and posted content from your account
  • Instagram Comments: Comments on your posts, including commenter usernames and comment content
  • Saved Posts: Posts you have saved to your Instagram collections (if you grant this permission)

Email Data

  • Gmail Messages: We access your Gmail inbox to read emails from brands, retailers, and newsletters (filtered by keywords like "brand", "newsletter", "new arrivals", "sale") sent within the last 24 hours. This helps us identify new product releases and promotional offers relevant to your audience.
  • Email Content: Subject lines, sender information, and email body text from brand/retailer emails

Trend Analysis Data

  • Social Media Trends: We collect publicly available trending topics from YouTube and TikTok to provide context for product recommendations
  • Product Information: Brand names, product categories, prices, and URLs from publicly available sources and brand emails

Communication Information

  • Email Address: Your email address for receiving automated product recommendation reports
  • Service Usage: How often you use the Service, features accessed, and automation run history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following specific purposes:

Product Sourcing and Trend Analysis

  • Identify Trending Products: Analyze your Instagram DMs, Instagram comments, email communications, TikTok content, and YouTube videos to identify products and brands that are popular, requested by your followers, or being promoted by brands
  • Detect Shopping Intent: Recognize when your audience is expressing interest in purchasing specific items or product categories through DMs and comments
  • Monitor New Releases: Track new product launches and sales from brand emails in your Gmail inbox
  • Identify Trends Across Platforms: Cross-reference trending topics from YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram to ensure product recommendations align with current social media trends
  • Determine Weekly Themes: Generate "Shopping Intention" themes based on patterns across all data sources - your DMs, comments, brand emails, and social media trends (e.g., "Workwear Staples," "Spring Beauty Favorites")
  • Generate Recommendations: Provide 5-12 curated product recommendations that match your audience's interests, current trends, and brand offerings

AI Processing

  • Automated Analysis: Process Instagram DM content, Gmail brand emails, YouTube trending data, and TikTok trending data through AI language models (accessed via OpenRouter platform) to extract product mentions, brand names, new releases, trending topics, and shopping intent
  • Natural Language Understanding: Analyze the context and sentiment of messages, emails, and social media trends to understand what products would resonate with your audience
  • Pattern Recognition: Identify recurring themes and trending topics across all data sources - Instagram DMs and comments, brand emails, YouTube videos, and TikTok content
  • Cross-Platform Correlation: Connect trends appearing across multiple platforms to identify the most relevant and timely product recommendations
  • AI Model Providers: We use OpenRouter to access various AI models including Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, OpenAI GPT-4, Meta Llama, and others. We may add or change models to improve service quality.

Report Generation

  • Daily Reports: Compile and send automated email reports with product recommendations
  • Product Descriptions: Generate AI-powered product descriptions answering: Would your audience buy this? What problem does it solve? Why was it chosen?
  • Contextual Insights: Provide reasoning for product selections based on DM requests, brand email promotions, and social media trends

Service Improvement

  • Improve the accuracy of trend detection
  • Enhance product recommendation quality
  • Optimize automation workflows
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Data Storage

Workflow Processing Platform: We use N8N Cloud (n8n.io), a workflow automation platform hosted on Microsoft Azure infrastructure in Frankfurt, Germany (Azure Germany West Central data center). This ensures GDPR compliance and EU-based data residency.

What Gets Stored and For How Long:

  • Workflow Execution Data: When your automation runs, N8N temporarily stores execution logs that include Instagram DM content processed during that run. This data is used for workflow monitoring, debugging, and error tracking. Execution data is automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Instagram DM Content: Your DM data passes through our workflow for real-time analysis but is NOT permanently stored in a separate database. It exists only in temporary execution logs and is automatically purged after 30 days.
  • Gmail Brand Email Content: Brand emails are processed in real-time during workflow execution. Email content exists only in temporary execution logs for the same 30-day period, then is automatically deleted.
  • AI Processing (OpenRouter): When DM content is sent to AI services for analysis, it is processed in real-time and not stored by the AI providers (per their privacy policies). Only the analysis results (trends, product recommendations) are retained.
  • Product Recommendations: The output of our analysis (product lists, recommendations, shopping intentions) are sent via email.
  • Account Information: Your email address and Instagram connection details are retained while your account is active and for 90 days after disconnection.

Important Note: While we do not intentionally store Instagram DM content long-term, N8N Cloud's execution history system does temporarily retain this data as part of workflow logs. You can request immediate deletion of specific execution data at any time.

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to data, and access is logged
  • API Security: All API connections use OAuth 2.0 authentication and access tokens with limited permissions
  • No Human Review: Your DMs are processed entirely by automated AI systems; no humans manually read your messages

Where Your Data is Processed

  • N8N Cloud: Workflow automation and temporary execution data storage (Frankfurt, Germany - Azure Germany West Central data center - GDPR compliant)
  • OpenRouter & AI Model Providers: AI processing and natural language analysis (Various global locations depending on model provider - see OpenRouter Privacy Policy)
  • Facebook/Instagram Graph API: Data retrieval from your Instagram account (United States - Meta infrastructure)
  • RapidAPI Services: Social media trend analysis (Global CDN)
  • Gmail API: Email delivery for reports (United States - Google infrastructure)

Data Encryption:

  • In Transit: All data transfers use TLS/SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • At Rest: N8N Cloud uses Azure Storage server-side encryption with AES-256 (FIPS-140-2 compliant)
  • Credentials: All API keys and OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest in N8N's secure database

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can request a copy of data we have collected about you by emailing fginsights4@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Access Request."

If you request data access, we can only provide data that exists within active execution logs at the time of your request. In most cases, only recent data (within the last 30 days) will be available.

Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To delete your data:

  • Email us: Send a request to fginsights4@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Data"
  • Include: Your Instagram username and the email address associated with your account
  • Timeline: Data is automatically deleted from N8N execution logs after 30 days. We cannot expedite this deletion as it follows N8N Cloud's automatic purging schedule. Upon receiving your request, we will immediately disconnect your Instagram and Gmail accounts to stop all future data collection.
  • Confirmation: You will receive email confirmation once your accounts are disconnected and when the 30-day retention period has expired

Disconnect Your Instagram Account

To disconnect your Instagram account and stop data collection:

  • Via Meta Business Suite: Go to Meta Business Settings → Data Sources → Instagram Accounts → find your connected account and remove API access
  • Via Email: Contact us at fginsights4@gmail.com with subject line "Disconnect Instagram" and include your Instagram username
  • This immediately stops all Instagram data collection
  • Existing Instagram data in N8N execution logs will be automatically deleted after 30 days

Disconnect Your Gmail Account

To revoke Gmail access and stop email data collection:

  • Via Google: Go to Google Account Permissions → find "FG Insights Product Sourcing Agent" and click "Remove Access"
  • Via Email: Contact us at fginsights4@gmail.com with subject line "Disconnect Gmail"
  • This immediately stops all Gmail data collection
  • Existing email data in N8N execution logs will be automatically deleted after 30 days

Correct Your Data

If any information we have about you is incorrect, you can request corrections by emailing fginsights4@gmail.com.

Opt-Out of Emails

You can stop receiving automated product recommendation emails by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email
